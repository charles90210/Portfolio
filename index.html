About Me

I am a grad student at the University of San diego majoring in Cyber Security Engineering. Grew up in south-eastern Nigeria but live in sunny San Diego now. Love computers and anything that involves working with them. My passion for creativity and the power of coding to bring ideas to life drew me to software development and I have never looked back. In addition to coding, my interests are in web/mobile application development, application security and design. Enjoy delivering projects that improve lives and make a positive impact. When I am not coding, I spend my time pursuing my other passions which include running, traveling, volunteering and gaming.

I'm currently looking for full-time and internship opportunities in software development and/or application security.

Learn Academy Web Development Bootcamp

Full-Stack Web development Student• September 2016

Learn academy is an intensive 3-months web development bootcamp that teaches students through intensive classroom training and hands on group projects, the fundamentals of web and application development and how to succeed as software developers in the tech/IT industry. Students are taught and exposed to working with different web development langugaes and frameworks like Ruby on Rails, HTML/CSS, JavaScript, Ruby, jQuery, AJAX, object oriented programming and development and how to collaborate on teams and projects using git/github, pair programming and the agile workflow. Students graduate at the end of the program equipped to hit the ground running as full-stack software developers.
